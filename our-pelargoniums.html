Osis Pelargoniums

You can say that it's a family trait when we do something do it wholeheartedly. So of course, just buying flowers from others wasn't enough, we had to have our own. The other family trait is bad luck, so we chose the name ”Osis” as our name which means bad luck in Swedish.

Our Pelargoniums

Our goal as processors is to have pelargoniums that are strong growers, easy to maintain and have beautiful flowers. The categories of pelargoniums is of less importance for us as processors, we use everything from Zonarctic to Zonals in our processing.

Osis Barbapapa

Mother: PG Karin
Father: Unknown
First bloom: Summer of 2021

Simple but beautiful! This flower might at first sight look pretty plain but if you look really close you can see that the top of the flower leaf’s has a darker shade of pink than the other ones. This flower got her name Barbapapa because the color of the flower reminded us of the character Barbapapa we liked a lot as children.

Osis Rosa Himmel

Mother: Runes Valentin
Father: Unknown
First bloom: Summer of 2021

The flowers of this sort is really big and reminds us of the color of the sky in a summer evening. The name” Rosa Himmel” means pink sky in Swedish. This flower grows really, really fast!

Osis Fågeln Phoenix

Mother: Runes Valentin
Father: Unknown
First bloom: Summer of 2021

The color of this flower changes over time, the bud is really light but when time passes the color gets stronger and stronger. This one grows straight up so it’s important that you prune it to get a nice shape of the plant. When she first bloomed, we were struck by how grand and majestic she was. This made us think of The Phoenix Fawkes in Harry Potter.

Osis Vampyrella

Mother: Runes Valentin
Father: Unknown
First bloom: Summer of 2021

This one has really dark leaf’s almost black and in contrast to the strong color of the flower it looks really beautiful. This sort is a dwarf, this means it grows slow and doesn’t get so big. She reminded us of a vampire, ” Vampyrella” is the name of a girl vampire in a popular children’s show in Sweden.

Osis Flamingo

Mother: PG Karin
Father: Unknown
First bloom: Summer of 2021

Flamingo is one of our first own Pelargoniums. Don't ask why, but in some way this flower made us think of a flamingo. So that's what we chose to call it. She's a strong grower and you can place it in full sun or in wandering shadow it doesn't matter. This flower really likes to bloom!

Osis Rosa Pricken

Mother: Lara Passion
Father: Unknown
First bloom: Summer of 2021

Rosa Pricken is our very first own pelargonium. We were so happy when we saw the bud! We were less happy when the flower bud sprouted, it was ugly! But we really liked how this flower grows so chose to save it, maybe in the future this plant can be the mother of a flower that we actually like. ”Rosa Pricken” means pink dot in Swedish and as you can see the flower has a pink dot on every flower leaf.
